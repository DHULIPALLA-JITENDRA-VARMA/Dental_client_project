Dental Health Tips

Brush Properly

Brush your teeth at least twice a day with fluoride toothpaste for 2 minutes each time.

Floss Daily

Flossing removes plaque and food particles between teeth that brushing can't reach.

Regular Checkups

Visit your dentist regularly for cleanings and checkups to prevent problems.
